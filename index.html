From the Neighborhoods that Raised Us

The Detroit Roses Archive is a living, people-centered archive dedicated to preserving the stories, memories, and knowledge of Black Detroit. Rooted in the belief that future generations must understand what came before them in order to build what comes next, the archive serves as a historical reference for the generations that will inherit the city.
